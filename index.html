About Me

Hi, I'm Zoe — an interactive designer fuelled by curiosity, collaboration, and spirals of inspo.

I'm drawn to work that tells stories, shifts perspectives, and invites people in. Whether I'm designing UX flows, crafting immersive experiences, or experimenting with new tools, I'm always exploring how design can feel thoughtful, emotional, and a little unexpected.
